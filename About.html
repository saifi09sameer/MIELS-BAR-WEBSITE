Our Story

Set alongside the banks of River Thames, Miel's is a casual, one of a kind bistro offering a variety of comforting and original dishes. At Miel's, one can expect to be enthusiastically received by the restaurant’s young and dynamic team.

At the head of the team is the bistro's founder and head chef : Juel B who developed the menu with the idea to offer flavourful cooking that makes customers want to revisit.

The culinary experience at Miel's is enhanced by a courteous service and an appealing decor. The menu’s specialties, combined with a pleasant atmosphere, are sure to satisfy your longing for delectable food enjoyed in the good company of friends.
